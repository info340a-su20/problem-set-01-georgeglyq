Spider-Man

Spider-Man is a fictional superhero that first appeared in the comic book Amazing Fantasy. It is created by Steve Ditko and Stan Lee. It is also known as Peter Parker, with supernatural characteristics in agility, balance, power, speed, and reflexes.

  • Spider-Man always sacrifices himself for the sake of others.
  • Spider-Man is funny, humorous, and humble.
  • Spider-Man helps save the world and get rid of evil spirits, no matter what danger lies ahead.
